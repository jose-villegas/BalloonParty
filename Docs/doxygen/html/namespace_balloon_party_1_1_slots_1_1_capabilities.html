enum BalloonParty.Slots.Capabilities.PressureResponse

How a balloon reacts when a pressure-balance push reaches it.

The cascade is a chain of shoves between neighbours; this decides whether a balloon passes the shove along by stepping one cell, or gets out of the way entirely — and if so, where it heads.

Enumerator
ShoveNeighbour 

Displaces one cell to a neighbour, continuing the chain (the default balloon).

RelocateNearest 

Vacates to the nearest free slot, ending the chain — stays close (BubbleCluster).

RelocateFarthest 

Vacates to the farthest free slot, ending the chain — clears right out (Unbreakable).
